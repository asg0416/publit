# Design

아니근데 should feel like opening a quiet map of nearby thoughts, not like entering a feed or chat room.

## Principles

- The first screen is the usable thought map, not a marketing page.
- The brand signal is simple: 아니근데, 나만 이런 생각한 거 아니었네, and the selected range.
- Characters show tags on the map; body text appears only in detail.
- Empty states must still feel alive by showing hot tags and a clear first-thought CTA.
- Motion should be slow, ambient, and reduced for `prefers-reduced-motion`.

## Visual System

- White and off-white surfaces with thin pixel shadows and restrained point colors.
- No one-note purple, beige, espresso, or slate-only palette.
- The full map background is the primary surface.
- Cards are reserved for repeated items, sheets, dialogs, and individual controls.
- Buttons use at least 40px hit areas and tactile `scale(0.96)` press feedback.
